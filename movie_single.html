About

Anuraga Karikkin Vellam is a 2016 Indian Malayalam film directed by debutant Khalid Rahman. Starring Biju Menon and Asif Ali in lead roles, the film is produced by Shaji Nadesan, Prithviraj Sukumaran, Santhosh Sivan, and Arya under the banner of August Cinema. The film is realted to the life of a father and son and their life experience when son tries to breakup with his lover.
