About

Kammattipadam is a 2016 Indian Malayalam-language Action, Crime film Directed by Rajeev Ravi and written by P. Balachandran. The film stars Dulquer Salman, Vinayakan, Manikandan R Achari, Vinay Forrt, Shine Tom Chacko and features model Shaun Romy in her film debut. The songs were composed by K, John P. Varkey, Vinayakan, and Vishnu Vardhan. The film centers on Kammati Paadam, a slum locality in Ernakulam, Kerala. It focuses on how the Dalit community was forced to give up their lands to real-estate mafias. And how modern urbanization of Kochi metro-city took place over the plight of the Dalits
